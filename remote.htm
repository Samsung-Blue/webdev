Creating a safer world

DeTect sensor has the capability of transmitting data at a remote monitoring station. All the sensors work together to transmit the data at a central control room. Therefore there is no need for manual intervention at regions which are either inaccessible or exposed to radiation or high temperature.

This not only reduces the cost of industries, but also drastically reduces the chances for accidents at work places. Remote monitoring for long range pipe inspection can prove beneficial particularly for oil, gas and nuclear industries.
